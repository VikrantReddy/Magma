At our core is great content

HSG partners with and builds media platforms for the world’s most exciting companies.

Two beliefs govern our work. First, excellent content generates leads and grows businesses. Second, media is changing. The first wave of the internet was read only, then came the era of content creation and sharing. Today, content is currency, and companies are competing with each other for audiences. Businesses and their content are displacing traditional sources of knowledge and information.

We define “media platform” as a segment of your business that integrates with your core business, retaining and growing a clearly-defined audience, and driving profitable actions.
‍
And this content approach goes further: our clients become authorities and category-definers in their markets. This may mean going from no content production to thousands of active followers on LinkedIn in six months; or it might mean building out a New York Times-style microsite complete with original research.

Ways to work with us

Our services are designed to help you reach your strategic aims, utilising content to strengthen and grow your core business. We are flexible in how we work, and can adapt all of our services, and the relationship we’ll build with you, to meet your needs.

Content strategy & creation

We write, record and produce excellent content, planning, testing and iterating as we go.

Optimisation & measurement

We help you distribute this content, measuring its impact to ensure it’s growing your business.

Full media platform build-out

We create a complete content function, integrated with and enriching your core business.

How we help

We focus on building out full media platforms and content functions for our clients. This includes producing:

Thought leadership: op-eds, blogs, articles, acting as thought partners to leadership.
Strategic pieces: reports, papers, bids, books and large strategic projects.
Fundraising materials: decks, prospectuses, DDQs and data room build-out.
Product and operational content: sales and marketing documents, in-product and web copy, and transcreation across all European languages.
Day-to-day owned content: case studies, product guides, factsheets, boilerplates, interviews, Q&As and press releases.
Support content: brand and copy guidelines, style guides and company-wide – or team-specific – templates, boilerplates and decks.

And we’ll also help you with:

  • Existing content optimisation
  • Digital and product marketing support
  • Social media strategy and community management
  • Earned content distribution (i.e. media)
  • Messaging and positioning work across the business. We approach messaging via product-market fit and growth metrics, not through brainstorms and post-its. 
  • Complementary advisory, particularly around fundraising
  • Full partnership advisory and network-building. HSG has a deep financial and tech-based network, which we are more than happy to tap into for clients.
  • Talent advisory and acquisition management
