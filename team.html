Ex-journalists and content strategists ready to make content your competitive advantage

Florence Robson

Mickaël Ruffinoni

Writer. Mickaël is a specialist copywriter and marketer, and HSG’s lead transcreator, working closely with clients rolling out in European markets.

Niamh Cassidy

Writer and Project Lead. Niamh is former head of content at fintechs Onfido and Fidel API. She manages, as well as writes for, projects with corporate clients like Jacobs, and our VC clients like Dawn Capital and Connect Ventures.

Josh Davis

Writer and Project Lead. Josh has written books with entrepreneurs and business leaders, developed messaging for start-ups and funds, and authored numerous thought leadership reports. For HSG, he writes white papers for clients including infrastructure giant Jacobs and fintech Moonfare, as well as columns for general partners, founders and CEOs.

Lynsey Barber

Writer. Lynsey is a freelance journalist, former technology editor of City AM and was chief editor at a financial wellbeing startup. She works across the portfolio, focusing on editorial content for the likes of Curve and Crowdcube.

Michael Lane

Writer. Michael is former content lead at Klarna, and Fidelity before that. He works closely with our fintech, finance and investment clients.

Emily Horton

Saidat Giwa-Osagie

Lois Neville

Steve Dinneen
